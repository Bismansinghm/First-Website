Bachelors of Computer Science




Bachelor of Computer Science [BCS]:
BCS is a three-year undergraduate course that covers topics like hardware, digital electronics, discrete mathematics, computer science, and more. Candidates interested in taking this course should check to see if they meet the BCS Course Eligibility requirements. Additionally, the Bachelor of Computer Science is referred to as the BCS Full Form. The BCS program lasts for three years. The BCS admissions procedure is based on merit.

BCS Eligibility is one of the following:
Minimum 45% (relaxable for reserved categories) in Class 12 with mathematics from a recognized board Relevant and recognized diploma course after Class X
BCS Eligibility:

The aspirants who are interested in studying the course of BCS should make sure to possess enough eligibility as per the specified criteria. The eligibility criteria differ from institute to institute. Furthermore, the BCS Eligibility Criteria is that the candidates must have secured a minimum of 45% from a recognized board.

Why study BCS?
One of the most in-demand areas of computer science is software development, which will continue to rule the job market. Graduates are guaranteed a larger earning potential as the years go by due to the increased demand and prominence of their field. A number of practical sessions are included in the bachelor of computer science programme to help students develop the necessary experience. They frequently receive competitive pay scales due to their extensive professional expertise and challenging job responsibilities.

How is B.C.S Course Beneficial?
Students can pursue further studies in M.Sc in Computer Science or M.C.A ( Computer Science), M.C.A, M.C.M, M.B.A and follow it up by research or take up jobs as trainee programmers with a bright future ahead. A Bachelor of Computer Science degree is normally required in order to work in the field of software development.

How to Apply?
Admission to the BCS course can happen through an entrance examination and is merit-based. The candidates are required to register themselves on the official website of the college or provide a filled hard copy of the registration form within the deadline date. Certain Private colleges have a direct admission method where the students are called for counselling sessions based on their application form and undergraduate scores. Candidates need to fill out the required details according to the existing documents.

Advantages or Benefits of Bachelor of Computer Science Course:
The advantages or benefits of a bachelor's degree in computer science The benefits of a bachelor's degree in computer science are tremendous. Students learn about common programming languages, data structures, computer organization, and design, among other topics, in the course study. The course's numerous higher study options are another advantage. After completing the course, one can look for a lucrative career in the computer science and technology industry of today. Jobs in the IT industry pay well to people who are skilled and hardworking.
